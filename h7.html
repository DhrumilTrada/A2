Frequently Asked Questions

Most common questions and answers related to the media downloader.
If you’re still not finding what you need, chat with us or visit our Help Center.

Is this tool free?

Yes! This tool is completely free. You don't need a Publer account to use it.

How does this tool work?

Simply go to the post you would like to download the media content from, copy the URL from your browser address bar into the input field, and press the download button. On success, you will be able to download the photos and videos from the post to your device, or share them across your social accounts via Publer.

What channels does this tool support?

This tool supports photos and videos from all major social networks and media-sharing sites. If you can access a photo or a video in incognito mode, so should this tool. Please contact us if you're having issues with a specific URL.

What is the quality of the downloaded photos and videos?

We will download photos and videos in their highest quality possible.

Is downloading public photos & videos from the internet legal?

Downloading copyrighted photos and videos without permission is illegal. It's also against the law when you share these files with someone else. Please use this tool at your best judgement. We're not responsible for your actions.
